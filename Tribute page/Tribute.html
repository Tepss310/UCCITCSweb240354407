Burritos

About my comfort food:

  • The word "burrito" means "little donkey" in Spanish, possibly because burritos can carry a lot of fillings like a donkey carries loads.
  • Burritos originated in northern Mexico and became popular in the United States in the 20th century, especially in California and Texas.
  • Traditional burritos are made with a flour tortilla wrapped around ingredients like rice, beans, meat, cheese, and salsa.
  • There are regional variations of burritos, including breakfast burritos, wet burritos smothered in sauce, and even sushi-inspired burritos.
